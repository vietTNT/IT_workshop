How learning at Shinra Tensei will help you improve your English skill?

When practicing at shinratensei.com, you will go through four main steps to enhance communication skill.

1. Listen an audio

Listening a casual conversation between 2 or many people.

2. Shadowing

When listening, practicing speaking skill by using Shadowing method

3. Pronounce

Focus on IPA, writing down a new word and pronounce it repeatedly.

4. Practice with AI

Virtual realistic experience, taking a conversation with AI, trying to speak English naturally as much as possible.
